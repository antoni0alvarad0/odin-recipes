Cranberry Sauce Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • 2 Tbsp of fresh orange juice (fornt the zested orange)
  • 1/4 cup of crystallized ginger candy (cut these into match- stick strips)
  • 4 cups of fresh cranberries (one 12ozbag)

Instructions

  • Bring the cup of water, sugar and salt to a boil in a medium sauce pot over medium-high heat.
  • Once at a boil add the remaining ingredients and stir to combine them all.
  • After all the ingredients have been combined reduce to a simmer and cook, stirring occasionally, untill cranberries burst and liquid thickness slightly. This usually takes about 10 minutes.
  • Once the cranberries have burst, stir the suace remove from heat and place the sauce in a serving dish and serce warm or room temperature with turkey, turkey burgers/sandwiches, or on pork loin.
