Muchines

The perfect morning or afternoon coffee companion

Muchines are a food that is mostly eaten in Ecuador in the mornings due to its high carbs content it holds workers through the morning into the afternoon.

This recipe was left to me by my grandmother she use to make them in the morning and just sometimes she would make them later on in the evening as a little snack just for us it was great.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of Yuca
  • Tropical frying cheese
  • Scallions
  • Salt
  • Avocado oil

Steps

  1. Cut and take the skin off the yuca after doing so cut the yuca into smaller more controllable pieces.
  2. You will be grading the pieces of yuca. Once you have graded all the pieces of yuca you can place the graded yuca into a large bowl.
  3. Dice the scallions and half of the block of cheese into small dices. This will be incorporatedd with the yuca in the bowl, add salt for taste. Make sure all ingredients are well mixed together.
  4. In a frying pan place enough oil to cover the bottom of the pan.
  5. While the oil is heating up form small yuca football style ball and set aside till oil heats up.
  6. Once oil is hot place muchines in the oil and fry till you get Golden Brown and Delicious on all sides and cooked fully through.
