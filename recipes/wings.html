Korean Style Chicken Wings

Great any time put BEST during a game

This is a recipe that came together during a late night poker game, it was late and there was not many things open to order from. So we had a bag of chicken wings, straight to defrost

I took out some asian ingredients that I had in the fridge and put together a sauce that would compliment the wings in an asian infusion way. I will say for something that what so fast it worked out really well taste was on point.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of wings
  • Soy sauce
  • Housin sauce
  • Korean paste
  • Scallions
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Place all wet ingredients in a bowl and combine them and set aside. Cut up all scallions into thin rings also place to the side for garnish later.
  2. Dry wings, salt and pepper them in order to air fry them at 390 degrees for 22 minutes.
  3. While wings are cooking take wet ingredients and place them in a sauce pan, with stove top set to low simmer the sauce till thick and sticky.
  4. Once wings are done and to your desired crispiness, toss the wings in a large bowl with sauce and cover completely.
  5. Finally sprinkle the scallions on top as desired and serve hot.
